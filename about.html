The Gospel of Net ZeroGONZ is a Wholistic vision that symbolizes the global commitment to addressing climate change and achieving a state of "Net Zero". It is essentially a call to action rooted in the recognition that the urgent need to mitigate climate change by reducing emissions to a level where they are balanced by efforts to remove or offset an equivalent amount of greenhouse gases from the atmosphere.
This Gospel Of Net ZeroGONZ emphasizes the collective responsibility of individuals, communities, businesses, and nations to transition to sustainable practices, such as renewable energy, conservation, and carbon capture, to secure a more environmentally balanced and sustainable future for our planet moving forward.
